Hiker Tips

🎒

Pack Smart

Layer up, bring 2× the water you think you need, and always carry a first-aid kit and emergency whistle.

🗺️

Know Your Trail

Download offline maps before leaving. Check the weather and register with the local ranger station.

🍌

Fuel Right

Trail mix, bananas, energy bars, and a proper hot meal at camp. Food is morale on the mountain.

🌿

Leave No Trace

Pack out all rubbish. Stay on marked trails. Respect the wildlife and the plants along the way.

🌤️

Start Early

Hit the trail at first light. You beat the heat, the crowds, and get the best golden-hour light for photos.

👟

Gear Up

Break in boots before long hikes. Trekking poles save knees on the descent. Gaiters keep pebbles out.
